# Personal Preferences

Preferences holds your personal, per-user settings — your profile, the look of the app, and your usage. Open it from the **profile menu** (your avatar at the bottom of the sidebar) → **Preferences**.

## Account info

At the top, **Preferences** shows your **email** and your **role** (owner, admin, or member). These are read-only here — roles are managed by your admins.

## Profile

- **Avatar** — click **Upload** to set a profile image (up to 1 MB), or **Remove** to clear it. Without an image, CatalEx generates an avatar from your name.
- **Display Name** — the name shown across the app.

Click **Save Changes** to apply.

## Appearance

- **Mode** — switch between **Light** and **Dark**. (You can also toggle this quickly from the sidebar.)
- **Color Palette** — choose a theme. **Default** is the original CatalEx look; other options include *Rose Gold, Holographic, Deep Ocean, Northern Lights, Sunset, Midnight Chrome, Forest Canopy, Lavender Dream, Ember,* and *Glacier.* Selecting a palette applies it immediately.

## Usage statistics

Click **View Usage Statistics** to see your personal token usage — total, input, and output tokens.

## FAQ

**Can I change my role or email here?**
No. Email and role are shown for reference only. Role changes are handled by an admin (see [User Management](./user-management.md)).

**Do appearance settings sync across devices?**
Yes. Your mode and palette are saved to your account and follow you when you sign in elsewhere.
